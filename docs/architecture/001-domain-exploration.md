## Executive Summary

ProposalPilot is an AI-powered proposal and SOW engine targeting the $6.2T professional services market. Mid-market firms (50-500 employees) spend 20-40 hours per proposal with a 70-80% loss rate, burning $4,500-$9,000 in senior staff time per lost bid. No existing tool combines AI-native document intelligence, a self-learning knowledge base, and brand voice adaptation at mid-market pricing ($49-$999/mo).

The AI Sales Assistant Software market is $3.85B in 2026, growing to $26.09B by 2035 (23.7% CAGR). The proposal/RFP generation sub-segment is the fastest-growing at 57.2% CAGR.

## Key Strategic Recommendations

1. **Nail document parsing before anything else.** The #1 failure point for every competitor. Ship with fewer formats that work perfectly rather than many that are unreliable.
2. **Make the cold start painless.** Bulk import wizard + industry starter packs + "your first AI proposal in 30 minutes" north star.
3. **Conservative AI generation with citations.** Every claim cites a knowledge base source. Low-confidence sections flagged, not hidden.
4. **Creator-seat pricing, not per-user.** Reviewers, approvers, and viewers are free. Removes the adoption barrier plaguing Responsive/Loopio.
5. **Professional services vertical focus.** Purpose-built for agencies, consultancies, IT firms, and MSPs. Vertical depth beats horizontal breadth.
6. **Build the knowledge base flywheel.** Every completed proposal enriches the KB. Win/loss outcomes improve matching. The product gets smarter with use — this is the moat.
